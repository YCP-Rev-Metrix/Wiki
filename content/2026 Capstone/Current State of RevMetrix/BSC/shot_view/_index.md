### What this page is

This page shows the shot while it is running.

### What's on this page

- The left graph is the [Motor and SmartDot Graph Widget](../motor_graph_widget/).
- The right graph is the same [Motor and SmartDot Graph Widget](../motor_graph_widget/), configured for SmartDot signals.
- The Motor Graph displays instruction values at the time they finish sending, along with encoder values at that same time.
- Encoder lines exist for angle and tilt, but no encoder is connected so they read as `0`.
- When a shot is complete, you can save the shot or analyze it in [Analysis Mode](../analysis_mode/). You will still have the opportunity to save the shot in Analysis Mode.

### Warnings

> If navigation does not re-enable
> after the shot ends, press **E-Stop**.
